About Us

Based in Nagpur, India, the heart of Maharashtra's agricultural region, we're a leading exporter of premium rice, chickpeas, and pigeon peas. With 30 years of experience, we've honed our expertise in sourcing, processing, and delivering high-quality agricultural products to international markets. We're committed to quality, innovation, and sustainable agriculture, working closely with local farmers to ensure the best produce reaches our customers. Our reputation for reliability and excellence has made us a trusted partner in the global food trade.
